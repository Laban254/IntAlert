How to raise a complain

Raising a complaint with Internet Alert is a straightforward process designed to ensure your concerns are heard and addressed effectively. Follow these simple steps to report an issue on your preferred social media platform:

Create an Account:
To get started, you'll need to create an account on Internet Alert. This will allow you to submit and track your complaints.

Specify the nature of your complaint:
In as much detail as possible try to explain more about your complain. This may include the type of content, usernames involved, and any additional context that can help moderators understand the issue.

Insert the Links (if applicable):
If you have relevant screenshots or links to the problematic content, attach them to your complaint. This can provide crucial context for moderators.

Submit Your Complaint:
After filling out the necessary information, click the "Submit" button to send your complaint to the respective platform's moderation team.

Check Complain Status

You can track the status of your complaint within your Internet Alert account. This feature allows you to stay updated on any actions taken by the moderation team. Engage in Communication (if needed): In some cases, moderators may need additional information from you. Stay engaged with your Internet Alert account to ensure a smooth resolution process. Remember, your feedback plays a vital role in maintaining a safe and positive online environment. Internet Alert is here to support you in making a difference. If you have any questions or encounter any issues during the complaint submission process, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team for assistance.
